TomClansy'sTheDivision

This is a 3rd person view freeroam openworld shooter game. This game has a great main story line. The has had two free update missions to get EndGame gear. The game also has a zone in it called the Dark Zone that has NPC and PVP players in it. The game has been plagued with many glitches and bugs to cheat and manipulate the game to get gear and complete stuff easily. I personally hate the Dark Zone. The Dark Zone is mainly just getting killed by PVP players that have exploited all the glitches while they were available till they were patched to get the best stuff. Which makes it hard for anyone to have fun in there. That is why my rating is what it is.

I give this game a 3.5 of 5 star rating
