Tom Clansey's Rainbow Six Siege

So at the start of this I would like to say I love this game. I like that it is Team based 5 on 5. There are many different characters in the game called Operaters that you can chose from. Some of the operaters are in the scrolling banner up top. I personally like this game more then Call Of Duty Black Ops 3. I feel there is more of a strategy and a need to work together to win your matches. There is Bomb, Hostage, And Secure Area. Bomb is one team is attacking trying to defuse the Bomb and the other team is trying to keep you from disarming the bomb. These modes are all generally the same Hostage one team trys to capture and other defends the hostage. Secure is one team defends the other team from capturing the area.

I give this game a 4.5 of 5 star rating
